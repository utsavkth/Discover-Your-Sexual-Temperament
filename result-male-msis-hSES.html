Your Sexual Temperament Summary

Profile: Medium Inhibition (SIS) + High Excitation (SES)

Hey friend, your profile shows a high sensitivity to sexual cues — paired with a moderate tendency to hit the brakes when something feels off.

💡 What This Looks Like in Real Life

You're likely someone who feels desire often — maybe even strongly. A subtle cue, a romantic vibe, or physical closeness can switch things on fast. Your body says “yes” to the right signals, and your SES (sexual accelerator) fires up easily.

But if something in the context doesn’t sit right — tension, disconnection, performance pressure — your SIS (the brake system) starts whispering, and desire might fizzle. This isn’t dysfunction. It’s your nervous system saying: “Wait. Are we safe here?”

🧠 For Men Like You

Let’s get honest — you’ve probably been told that men should “always be ready,” or that high arousal = high performance. But your system is more layered. You may want sex, but not feel fully present for it. You may get turned on but struggle to stay tuned in. And that can be frustrating.

But this profile is powerful. It means your arousal is rich, responsive, and sensitive — but it also asks for emotional attunement. You’re not a robot. You’re a real human being with layers.

✅ Ways to Support Your Sexual Style

  • 🎧 Set the tone: music, lighting, comfort — arousal flows best in environments that help you feel grounded and relaxed.
  • 🗣️ Talk it out: if something’s off, name it. Reducing low-grade stress can make space for your SES to shine.
  • 🕯️ Slow down: even if desire shows up fast, connection still takes time. Stay in the moment.
  • 📱 Be mindful of distractions: overstimulation (screens, pressure, rushed moments) can keep your body turned on but your mind checked out.
  • 💪 Shift from “perform” to “feel”: your worth isn’t in performance. It's in presence.
🔍 What do SIS and SES actually mean?

SIS (Sexual Inhibition System) is your internal brake. It watches for emotional, mental, or environmental signals that say “slow down.” Medium SIS means you're sensitive to pressure, disconnection, or stress — but you’re not ruled by it.

SES (Sexual Excitation System) is your gas pedal. It kicks in when you feel turned on, emotionally connected, or stimulated. A high SES means your body responds strongly to the right vibes — and wants more.

This combo is intense but sensitive. To thrive, it needs emotional clarity, safety, and trust — not just stimulation.
