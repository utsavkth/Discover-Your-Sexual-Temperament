Your Sexual Temperament Summary

Profile: Medium Inhibition (SIS) + Medium Excitation (SES)

Hey friend, your sexual system is steady, balanced, and quietly tuned-in. You’re not flooded by desire, and you’re not shut down by fear — you respond when the moment feels emotionally aligned, intimate, and genuine.

💡 What This Looks Like in Real Life

You don’t need intensity or performance to feel connected. A shared laugh, an emotionally safe space, or a calm night in can all gently spark your desire. But if something feels off — emotionally, energetically, or physically — your system will likely pause or slow down.

You may not always feel desire rise spontaneously, but when the conditions are right, you can feel fully present and engaged. You’re not driven by urgency. You’re driven by *authenticity* — and that’s a quiet superpower.

🌈 For Those Who Exist Beyond the Norms

If you don’t fit rigid gender, sexuality, or intimacy scripts, this profile is an invitation: you don’t have to push or perform. Your system is relational. Emotional tone matters to you. You show up when the connection feels mutual — not when it’s demanded.

Whether you identify as non-binary, gender-nonconforming, queer, neurodivergent — or simply *yourself* — your middle-ground rhythm is something to honor, not rush past.

✅ What Helps You Feel Most Like You

  • 🧠 Check in emotionally before physically: when you feel safe, seen, and calm, your SES opens up more fully.
  • 💬 Talk about what helps you stay tuned in: the more your partner understands your inner rhythm, the easier it is to co-create intimacy.
  • 🕯️ Curate context with intention: lighting, music, touch, shared rituals — these help you enter arousal naturally, without force.
  • 📓 Reflect gently: when did intimacy feel easy? When did it feel blocked? Your body is always telling you the truth.
  • 🎨 Let intimacy evolve: you’re not wired for repetition or chaos — you’re wired for growth, softness, and meaningful change.
🔍 What do SIS and SES actually mean?

SIS (Sexual Inhibition System) is your brake — it activates when something feels off, stressful, or emotionally unsafe. Medium SIS means you can pause and re-center when needed, but you’re not easily derailed.

SES (Sexual Excitation System) is your gas pedal — it responds to sensuality, emotional safety, and erotic cues. Medium SES means you can get there — but only when it feels right and grounded.

This combination is deeply balanced. You’re the kind of person who doesn’t chase intensity — you respond to realness. And that’s rare, resilient, and beautiful.
